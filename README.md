# cafe stop

A study escape to bring café vibes to your workspace.

Pick an ambience, start your Pomodoro timer, and get cozy with the sounds of a café — barista chatter, coffee machines, and soft background music — all while you focus on your work.

## about

During the pandemic, going out to study wasn’t really possible. As someone who loves background ambiance, I created Cafe Stop to recreate that café feeling wherever you are!
